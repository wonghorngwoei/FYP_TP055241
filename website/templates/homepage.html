About LDPMS!

Welcome to Lifestyle Disease Prediction & Management System, an advanced healthcare solution empowering individuals in predicting and managing lifestyle diseases. Our system offers personalized health insights and actionable recommendations for informed decisions by utilizing data analytics and machine learning algorithms.

Built with Python and Flask, we ensure a seamless user experience. Additionally, our integration of Google Places API helps users find nearby hospitals.

Join us on this journey to a healthier and happier lifestyle!
